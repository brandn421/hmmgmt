Learn about our mission.

H&M Management has been operating apartment communities and consulting in the real estate industry since 1975.

It is our mission to provide the best value in the apartment industry, offering quality housing at affordable pricing.

A private family owned business, the managing partner is Martin Cohen. Mr. Cohen is a lifetime trustee of the Northeast Ohio Apartment Association, served as President in 1996. Mr. Cohen's career includes earning a master degree in finance, formerly a professor at Case Western Reserve University (1977-1983), officer at Ameritrust (1977-1979), been a consultant to Broadview Savings and Loan Co. (1982-1986), and currently a Board of Director to Third Federal Savings and Loan.


The company prides itself with stability, with its low tenant turnover and long term employees. H&M Management treats its staff members as family and preaches to treat each other as they would want to be treated, with respect!
